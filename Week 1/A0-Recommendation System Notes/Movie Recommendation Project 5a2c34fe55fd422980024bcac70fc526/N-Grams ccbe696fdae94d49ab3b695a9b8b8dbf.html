N-Grams

  • continuous sequences of words or symbols or tokens in a document.
  • neighboring sequences of items in a document
    • 1 → Unigram
    • 2 → Bigram
    • 3 → Trigram
    • n → n-gram
  • Different types of problem requires different value of N
  • For span filtering, trigrams or 4-grams seems to be the best fit
